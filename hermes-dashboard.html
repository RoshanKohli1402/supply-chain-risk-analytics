Model Interpretation — What Drives Late Delivery?
BUSINESS TRANSLATION OF SHAP FINDINGS
🚢
Shipping Mode = 45% of Prediction Power
The single biggest predictor of late delivery is which shipping mode is chosen. Second Class = almost guaranteed delay. Standard Class = near-zero delay risk.
📅
Scheduled Window = 32% Influence
Longer planned shipping windows correlate with higher late risk — suggesting systemic underestimation of fulfillment time in order processing.
👤
Customer Segment = 15% Influence
Customer type (Consumer vs Corporate vs Home Office) drives 15% of delay prediction — suggesting differential treatment in fulfillment priority.
